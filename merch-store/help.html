SHIPPING INFO

We ship worldwide from our fulfillment centers in Asia and Europe. Orders are typically processed within 2–3 business days.

  • Standard Shipping: 7–14 business days (Free over $80)
  • Express Shipping: 3–5 business days via DHL/FedEx

Tracking information will be sent to your email as soon as the package is dispatched.

RETURNS & EXCHANGE

Your satisfaction is our priority. If your jersey doesn't fit or has a defect, you can return it within 30 days of delivery.

Conditions for return:

  • Item must be unworn and unwashed.
  • All original tags must be attached.
  • Customized items (if applicable) cannot be returned unless defective.

To start a return, please contact our support team at support@oldblood.gg.
